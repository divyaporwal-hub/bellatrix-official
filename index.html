About


"If women had been more prominently talked about in computing , both in the history book and schools, we literally would not have the lack of women programmers that we do today" --Reshma Saujani

Bellatrix which is a Latin word that means Female Warriors. The community aims at ensuring equal access to technology to close the gender digital divide.This community will provides opportunities to gain digital literacy, which would able to grow their learning, expand their information source and communicate with others.
